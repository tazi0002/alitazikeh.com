About Me

Creative work grounded in kindness, curiosity, and reliable collaboration.

I am an Interactive Media Design student at Algonquin College who enjoys connecting with people through creative projects. I value kindness, respect, and positive collaboration, and I bring a calm, friendly energy into every environment I work in.

I am naturally curious and have a strong passion for learning new skills, exploring new ideas, and challenging myself to grow both personally and professionally. I take pride in being reliable, supportive, and easy to work with, and I believe that strong relationships and clear communication are just as important as strong results.

I enjoy working with others, sharing ideas, and contributing to projects where creativity and teamwork come together. Whether in a classroom, a team setting, or a professional environment, I aim to create a positive experience for the people around me.
